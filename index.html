The Core Conflict: Choose Your Future

Old World Path (Restoration)

Focuses on restoring high-efficiency, complex **modern infrastructure** using salvaged components. **Trade-off:** High density and efficiency, but dependent on non-renewable salvage and high maintenance.

New World Path (Adaptation)

Focuses on low-tech, self-sustaining structures using **local, renewable materials**. **Trade-off:** High resilience and low maintenance, but lower efficiency and population density.

The Reclamation Loop

A multi-tiered **recycling mechanic** where every piece of old-world junk must be meticulously disassembled, refined, or upcycled into usable building materials.

Scenario Showcase: Variable Apocalypses

High replayability is achieved through selectable starting scenarios that fundamentally alter resource availability and primary threats.

The Desertion (Climate/Drought Collapse)

The struggle is against **Water Scarcity, Heat, and Sand**. Logistics must prioritize cooling and highly efficient irrigation over all else.

The Contamination (Fungal/Viral Collapse)

The struggle is against **Disease, Spores, and Tainted Resources**. Focus shifts to Sanitation, Filtration, and organized defense.
